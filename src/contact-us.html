Phone

612-373-3333

One number to reach all Metro Transit services.

NexTrip

NexTrip provides automated, real-time information 24 hours a day for trips departing in the next 20 minutes and scheduled times thereafter. You can also access it at metrotransit.org/nextrip.

Transit Information Center

Speak with a transit expert about routes, schedules and fares. Open 6:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. weekdays and 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on weekends and holidays. Closed Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.

Customer Relations

Customer Relations staff are available to answer questions and take comments Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. 

Lost & Found

Talk to a Customer Relations specialist to report items left on a bus or train. If staff locate the item, they will mail it to you or make arrangements for you to pick it up at Metro Transit (570 Sixth Avenue North, Minneapolis see map).

Go-To Card balance

Check your card's current balance or add value. You can also add value here.

By TTY

711 – Minnesota Relay (hearing impaired)

Text

612-444-1161 | Text for Transit Information

Text your general transit questions or trip planning requests to 612-444-1161, and a transit expert will get back to you within minutes with the answers. Available whenever the Transit Information Center is open: 6:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. weekdays and 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on weekends and holidays. Closed Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.

612-900-0411 | Text for Safety

If you see something suspicious or inappropriate on a bus or train or at a transit station or stop, Text for Safety to silently report it to Metro Transit. Please include route, bus or train number, location and the issue. If you witness a crime or have an emergency, please call 9-1-1.

This service is also available via the Metro Transit Mobile App.

Metro Transit Police TipLine

612-349-7222

If you witnessed a crime and you're able to provide details that could help police, please use this number to report the issue.

Service Center

Visit the Metro Transit Service Center in downtown Minneapolis or downtown St. Paul for trip planning assistance, to pick up printed schedules or to buy or add value to a Go-To Card.
