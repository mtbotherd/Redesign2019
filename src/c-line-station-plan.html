Station plan

The Metropolitan Council has approved a C Line Station Plan to finalize locations for enhanced C Line stations before detailed engineering began in 2016. The C Line Station Plan establishes two core station components: the station intersection and the location of platforms within that intersection. Some preliminary plans for other details (e.g. curb bump-outs, platform length, shelter improvements) are provided in the plan, but details are finalized throughout the detailed design and engineering phase in 2016-2017. See characteristics of a typical station.

The final C Line Station Plan document identifies C Line station locations approved by the Metropolitan Council on April 27, 2016. Draft and recommended versions of the plan can be found within the Project Library.

Read the complete final C Line Station Plan (approved Apr. 27, 2016) or see individual station plans below.
