C Line construction

Metro Transit has coordinated with several partner agencies to construct the C Line with other infrastructure projects.

Sign up to receive construction bulletins

Penn Avenue improvements project

The Penn Avenue Improvements Project is a collaborative effort by Metro Transit, Hennepin County and the City of Minneapolis to make meaningful infrastructure improvements and enhance transportation and transit service and safety throughout Penn Avenue and Olson Memorial Highway (Hwy. 55) into downtown Minneapolis. Construction led by Metro Transit will begin in spring 2018 and continue through early 2019. Read more about the Penn Avenue Improvements Project.

Major construction on Penn Avenue is complete. There is ongoing construction activity on C Line stations that may require temporary lane restrictions or sidewalk detours through the Spring of 2019.

Please refer to rider alerts to learn of any changes at bus stops along Penn.

Brooklyn Boulevard reconstruction

Hennepin County and the City of Brooklyn Center will reconstruct a 1.4-mile stretch of Brooklyn Boulevard (County Road 152) from 49th Avenue North to Bass Lake Road (County Road 10) in Brooklyn Center. Construction began on August 23 and will continue through the fall of 2019. The Brooklyn Boulevard & 51st Avenue Station will be constructed as part of the Brooklyn Boulevard Reconstruction Project.

Visit the City of Brooklyn Center website for weekly updates.

Brooklyn Center Transit Center and Ramp A/ 7th St Transit Center

Metro Transit will make improvements to the Brooklyn Center Transit Center (BCTC) at the northern terminus of the C Line to ready the transit center for rapid bus service:

  • Upgrading communications equipment for additional NexTrip real-time information screens
  • Reconstructing portions of concrete bus lane panels for extend the life and provide better ride quality.
  • Outfitting future C Line and D Line gates for off-board ticketing equipment, NexTrip real-time information signs and bus arrival annunciators.

Construction will also occur at the Ramp A/7th St Transit Center in 2018, and will include:

  • Upgrading communications equipment for additional NexTrip real-time information screens
  • Outfitting the waiting area with off-board ticketing equipment, NexTrip real-time information signs and bus arrival annunciators.

Stay updated on impacts to bus service at these locations through rider alerts.

Olson Memorial Highway temporary stations

When the C Line opens in 2019, it will serve temporary stations on Olson Memorial Highway. These temporary stations will be built in 2018 and will be affected during planned construction of the METRO Blue Line Extension. After the METRO Blue Line Extension opens for service on Olson Highway and C Line stations are built on Glenwood Avenue, the permanent alignment of the C Line will shift to Glenwood Avenue.

8th Street stations

C Line stations on 8th Street will be built as part of the 8th Street Reconstruction Project in 2019-2020. During the reconstruction project, most bus stops on 8th Street will be closed. C Line buses will drop customers off at stops on 6th Street.
