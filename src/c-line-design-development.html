Design Development

Click on a station name to look through the images of each station. This map is best viewed on a desktop computer. You may experience limited functionality with this content on a mobile device.

Detailed design and engineering of the C Line is expected to last throughout 2017. Pending full project funding, C Line construction is expected to begin in 2018. Revenue service would start in 2019.
